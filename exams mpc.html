Top 10 Engineering Entrance Exams in INDIA - In Detail

The top engineering entrance exams in India are listed below for the reference of all the aspirants who wish to pursue engineering. These engineering entrance test are highly popular to get admission in top engineering colleges in India. More details are provided under each examination for the benefit of the students.

JEE Mains

National Testing Agency (NTA) will conduct the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Main 2021 four times this year - February, March, April & May. JEE Mains 2021 will be held in computer based mode (except the drawing section of Paper 2 (B.Arch) which is a pen and paper based exam). JEE Main is the admission procedure into the well-known IIITs, NITs and other more prestigious engineering institutes of the nation. More than 12 lakh applicants appear for this examination hoping to clear and reach JEE Advanced for admissions. The duration of the exam is 3 hours long and is multiple choice in nature. The examination is divided into two papers – Paper 1 is for B.Tech/B.E and Paper 2 is for B.Arch or B.Planning.

There are two things that candidates can look for after the JEE Main result is declared. JEE ADVANCED if they are amongst the 2,50,000 students who have scored equal to or above the JEE Main cut-off. The second step is admissions through JOSAA or state.

JEE Advanced

It is conducted by one of the seven zonal IITs (IIT Roorkee, IIT Kharagpur, IIT Delhi , IIT Kanpur, IIT Bombay, IIT Madras, and IIT Guwahati) under the guidance of the Joint Admission Board (JAB). It is the sole prerequisite for admission to the Indian Institutes of Technology.The examination is organised each year by one of the IITs, on a round-robin rotation pattern. It has a very low qualification rate (about 9,369 in 479,651 in 2012; ~1.95%)

To get admission in IIT, students have to pass the JEE Exam : firstly, JEE Main Exam, secondly, JEE Advanced.

Lovely Professional University

Lovely Professional University (LPU) is a private university situated in Phagwara, Punjab, India. The university was established in 2005 by Lovely International Trust.This private University has over 20 schools that offer various diploma, UG, PG and doctoral courses in several streams, such as Engineering, Management, Science, Pharmacy, Arts, Law and more. Approved by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and a member of the Association of Indian Universities (AIU), LPU has received the ‘Best Private University Award’ by ASSOCHAM in 2017.

Andhra Pradesh Engineering, Agriculture, Medical Common Entrance Test

(AP EAMCET)

Andhra Pradesh Engineering, Agriculture, Medical Common Entrance Test (AP EAMCET) is a state-level exam conducted by Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), Kakinada for admissions to Engineering, Agriculture and Medical courses offered at more than 425 private institutes in Andhra Pradesh state. JNTU Kakinada is only the exam conducting authority for AP EAMCET on behalf of the AP State Council of Higher Education (APSCHE). West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination ( WBJEE)

West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination or the WBJEE is a state-government controlled centralised test, conducted by the West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board for admission to many private and governmental engineering institutions in West Bengal.The test is taken after the 12th grade for admission to graduation (also known as Bachelors) courses. The exam can be taken by those who studied Pure Science and Vocational 10+2 stream in plus two level with the specific subjects tested in the examination, which are Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics.
